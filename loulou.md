## Authentication Checklist

### Quilted Leather

1. **Puffiness**: Authentic quilts are evenly puffed, not flat
2. **Alignment**: Quilts align across all panels
3. **Leather texture**: Soft, supple, consistent grain

### YSL Hardware

- **Authentic**: Heavy, substantial gold plating
- **Stamping**: "SAINT LAURENT" and "YSL" crisp and deep
- **Placement**: Correct centering on front flap

### Interior

- "SAINT LAURENT PARIS" gold foil stamp
- "Made in Italy" present
- Quality zipper

### Chain Strap

- Interwoven leather and chain
- Quality leather tabs
- "YSL" charm on end
