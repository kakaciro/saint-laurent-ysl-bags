## Authentication Guide 2026

### 1. Logo Hardware

The YSL logo is the #1 authentication point:

- **Authentic**: Crisp engraving, consistent depth, "SAINT LAURENT" below
- **Fake**: Shallow engraving, inconsistent plating

### 2. Chain Quality

- **Authentic**: Heavy, substantial feel
- **Fake**: Lightweight, hollow links

### 3. Leather Quality

- Soft lambskin with natural drape
- No chemical smell
- Consistent grain throughout

### 4. Stitching

- 10 stitches per inch
- Matching black thread
- Even throughout

### 5. Interior

- "SAINT LAURENT PARIS" stamp
- Made in Italy
- Quality zipper (YKK or Lampo)
