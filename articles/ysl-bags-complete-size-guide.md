# Saint Laurent YSL Bags: Complete Size Guide

## YSL Bag Collection Overview

Saint Laurent (formerly Yves Saint Laurent) offers a range of iconic bags. Understanding the sizing helps choose the right bag.

## Popular YSL Bag Sizes

### NIKI Baby
- Dimensions: 21 x 15 x 6 cm
- Shoulder strap drop: 45 cm
- Best for: Daily essentials, small essentials
- Chain strap fully adjustable

### NIKI Medium
- Dimensions: 28 x 20 x 8 cm
- Shoulder strap drop: 45 cm
- Best for: Work, everyday use
- Fits: Phone, wallet, keys, small cosmetics

### NIKI Large
- Dimensions: 36 x 26 x 10 cm
- Shoulder strap drop: 45 cm
- Best for: Travel, work laptop carry
- Fits: Tablet, documents, larger items

### LOULOU Small
- Dimensions: 27 x 18 x 9 cm
- Chain strap drop: 48 cm
- Best for: Evening, occasions
- Quilted羔羊皮 construction

### LOULOU Medium
- Dimensions: 35 x 25 x 12 cm
- Chain strap drop: 48 cm
- Best for: Daily use, work
- More structured than small

### LOULOU Puffer
- Sizes: Small (22cm), Medium (28cm), Large (34cm)
- Oversized绗缝设计
- Casual-chic aesthetic
- Very lightweight

### CASSANDRE Envelope
- Dimensions: 22 x 13 x 5 cm
- Wallet on chain style
- Card slots inside
- Best for: Evenings, minimal essentials

### JAMIE 4.10
- Dimensions: 28 x 18 x 8 cm
- Shopping tote style
- Fits: Laptop up to 13"
- Unstructured, casual

## Choosing the Right Size

### For Daily Work Use
- NIKI Medium or Large
- JAMIE 4.10 for more capacity
- LOULOU Medium for structure

### For Evening Events
- CASSANDRE Envelope
- LOULOU Small
- NIKI Baby

### For Travel
- JAMIE 4.10 or larger
- NIKI Large
- LOULOU Large
