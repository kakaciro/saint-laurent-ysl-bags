# YSL Sac de Jour: The Ultimate Luxury Business Bag

## YSL Sac de Jour Overview

The Sac de Jour represents Saint Laurent's interpretation of the perfect professional bag. Designed for functionality without sacrificing brand prestige, it has become a favorite among business professionals and fashion enthusiasts alike.

## Structure and Design

### Rigid Structure
Unlike softer leather bags, the Sac de Jour maintains its shape through structured side panels and reinforced handles. This architectural design ensures the bag holds its form even when empty.

### Interior Layout
- Main compartment with zip closure
- Interior zip pocket
- Two interior slots for cards and phone
- Suede lining throughout

## Leather and Hardware

### Grained Calfskin
The Sac de Jour uses premium grained calfskin that is both durable and water-resistant. The subtle grain texture helps hide minor scratches.

### Hardware
- YSL magnetic closure with Cassandre logo
- Padlock with key included
- Solid brass hardware in gold or silver finish

## How to Spot Authenticity

### Stitching
- Precise saddle stitching
- Consistent thread tension throughout
- Matches YSL's premium quality standards

### Serial Number
- Located on interior tag
- Heat-stamped, not printed
- Includes month/year code
