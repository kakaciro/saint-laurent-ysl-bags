# Saint Laurent YSL Leather Types: Complete Guide

## YSL Leather Materials

Saint Laurent uses premium leathers across their bag collection, each selected for specific bag styles and aesthetics.

## NIKI Leather

### Vintage Leather
- Supple calfskin with soft drape
- Semi-polished finish
- Develops patina over time
- Chain is lightweight aluminum

### Smooth Leather
- More structured feel
- Higher shine
- Shows light scratches
- Easy to condition

## LOULOU Leather

### Quilted Lambskin
- Ultra-soft羔羊皮
- Chevron or diamond quilting
- Very lightweight
- Delicate, prone to scratches

### Smooth Leather
- Structured silhouette
- Medium weight
- Casual luxury aesthetic
- More durable than quilted

## CASSANDRE Envelope Leather

### Smooth Calfskin
- Compact, fine grain
- Structured card holder style
- Minimal stretching
- Gold-tone YSL logo

## JAMIE Leather

### Smooth Leather
- Casual, unstructured tote
- Medium weight
- Practical and durable
- Fits laptop up to 13"

### Suede
- Soft, napped finish
- More casual aesthetic
- Requires protection spray
- Visible wear patterns

## LE 5 À 7 Leather

### Supple Smooth Leather
- Relaxed hobo silhouette
- Very soft drape
- Spacious interior
- Shoulder strap adjustable

## YSL Hardware Types

### Gold-Tone Brass
- Classic YSL Cassandre logo
- Substantial weight
- Durable plating
- Most common on dark leather

### Antique Silver
- Matte silver finish
- More casual aesthetic
- Onighter leather combinations
- Modern YSL collection

## Leather Care

### Regular Maintenance
- Wipe with soft dry cloth
- Apply leather conditioner quarterly
- Store in dust bag
- Avoid overfilling bags

### For Quilted Leather
- Avoid pressure to maintain quilting
- Professional cleaning recommended
- Do not apply thick conditioners
