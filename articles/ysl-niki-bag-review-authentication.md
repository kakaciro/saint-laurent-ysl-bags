# YSL Niki Bag: Complete Review and Authentication Guide

## The YSL Niki: Rock and Roll Elegance

The YSL Niki captures Saint Laurent's edgy aesthetic in a versatile single-chain bag design. Since its debut, it has become a staple for those who appreciate the brand's rock-and-roll heritage.

## Design Features

### Signature Cassandre Logo
The magnetic YSL closure features the iconic Cassandre logo, designed by Yves Saint Laurent's creative director Anthony Vaccarello. The closure is both functional and decorative.

### Chain and Leather Strap
The adjustable strap combines leather sections with polished metal chains, allowing multiple carrying styles:
- Shoulder bag
- Crossbody
- Top handle

## Leather Types

### Vintage Calfskin
The most popular option features a pre-distressed finish that hides scratches and develops beautiful patina over time.

### Smooth Leather
Clean and refined, smooth calfskin versions offer a more polished appearance but show wear more easily.

## Authentication Points

### Hardware Weight
Authentic YSL hardware has substantial weight. The Cassandre logo should be deeply engraved, not surface-printed.

### Stitching Quality
- Precise 45-degree angled stitching
- Consistent stitch length throughout
- No fraying or loose threads

### Interior Details
- Black suede lining
- YSL Made in Italy stamp
- Date code and authenticity badge
